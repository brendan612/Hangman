For my project I chose the classic game Hangman. Hangman is a very straight forward morbid game. You or your opponent thinks of a word, and the other one guesses. Too many wrong guesses, and the man gets hanged :( For this demonstration, you will be just playing against the computer.

As the guesser, the computer will choose from a large word bank, and you will have 7 turns to guess the word.
As the host, you can enter any word(s) up to 15 chars. total, and the computer will start guessing random letters. You have to hit good or bad to tell the computer if they guessed right.
